Per feature-area crosswalk. "Their variable group" lists the dominant
constructs from =define_labels= (programs.do:562-764) and the per-country
=.do=. "agg/item" is the dominant tag across the 7 overlap countries.
".do source" points at the construct to consult. "Recommendation" obeys both
cautions: aggregates/indices/ratios/median-valuations become
=transformations= functions over item-level data; data-layer features are
proposed at their natural grains (no wide mirror).

* 1. Panel IDs
- *Ours*: =panel_ids= (+ =updated_ids=), property-dicts on =Country=.
- *Theirs*: =hh_id_obs/_merge=, =indiv_id_obs/_merge=, =plot_id_obs/_merge=,
  =parcel_id_obs/_merge=, =manager_id_obs/_merge=, =respondent_id_obs/_merge=,
  =ea_id_obs/_merge=, =wave=, =season= (UGA). Grain: per-entity.
- *agg/item*: item (the =group()= relabels keys; for Mali/Niger the IDs are a
  cross-section "group()" so tagged agg, but they are still keys, not sums).
- *.do source*: =Append_{CC}.do= ID-frame section (e.g. =Append_ETH.do:30-49=,
  =Append_NGA.do:25=, =Append_UGA.do:130-136=).
- *Recommendation*: BOTH — no action. Our =panel_ids= already maps raw→panel.

* 2. Sampling/geography
- *Ours*: =sample= (i,t → v, weight, panel_weight, strata, Rural) +
  =cluster_features= (geovars, distances, aez).
- *Theirs*: =strataid=, =urban=, =admin_1..4 (+_name)=, =pw=,
  =geocoords_id/lat_modified/lon_modified=, =dist_road/popcenter/market=,
  =aez=, =elevation/twi/popdensity=. Grain: hh / ea.
- *agg/item*: item (=soil_fertility_index= is the one agg, a PCA — minor).
- *.do source*: =ETH_ESS1.do:111-178,1114-1175=; =NGA_GHS1.do:102-147,1172-1177=.
- *Recommendation*: BOTH — no action. =soil_fertility_index= (if wanted) is a
  =transformations= PCA over our soil-quality items, not a stored column.

* 3. HH composition

- *.do source*: =ETH_ESS1.do:957-961=; =MWI_IHPS1.do:965-976=; =TZA_NPS1.do:723-737=.
- *Recommendation*: BOTH^a — no data-layer action. =hh_dependency_ratio= is a

* 5. Education

* 6. Anthropometry/nutrition
- *Ours*: =nutrition= (Ethiopia; Uganda with FCT). Absent Mali/Malawi/Niger/
  Nigeria/Tanzania.
- *Theirs*: =weight=, =height= (item), =haz06/waz06/whz06/bmiz06= (z-score
  index), =wasting= (whz06<-2). Present ETH/MWI/NGA/TZA/UGA; absent MLI/NER.
  =HDDS= filed here too (dietary aggregate).
- *agg/item*: item for weight/height; z-scores are derived indices (agg) but
  per-individual.
- *.do source*: =ETH_ESS1.do:1217-1235=; =MWI_IHPS1.do:1213-1231=;
  =TZA_NPS1.do:1002-1024=; =NGA_GHS1.do:1296-1308=.
- *Recommendation*: THEIRS-ONLY for Malawi/Nigeria/Tanzania → *build
  =nutrition= as an item feature* at grain =(t,i,individual)= carrying raw
  weight/height; z-scores (haz/whz/...) are a =transformations= fn (anthro
  reference-pop lookup), NOT stored. See GAP_RANKING #2.

- *.do source*: =ETH_ESS1.do:1242-1264 (individual)=, =787-799 (plot days)=;
  =TZA_NPS1.do:1027-1062, 513-546=; =UGA_UNPS1.do:968-992, 467-509=.
- *Recommendation*: THEIRS-ONLY for ETH/MLI/MWI/NER/NGA/TZA → *build
  individual labor feature* =people_last7days= at =(t,i,individual)= (work
  dummies, hours, industry); *plot labor* is a separate feature
  =plot_labor (t,i,plot)= holding family/hired *days* (item rows, NOT the

  =transformations= over those. See GAP_RANKING #1.

* 9. Crop production/harvest
- *Ours*: NONE.

  =share_kg_sold= (ratio), =yield_kg= (harvest/area ratio), =main_crop=,

- *Recommendation*: THEIRS-ONLY^a all 7 → *build item feature*
  =crop_production (t,i,plot,crop)= holding *reported harvest quantity per
  crop+unit and reported sale quantity/value* (raw rows, not the plot-crop
  sum). =harvest_kg= (unit→kg sum), =yield_kg=, =share_kg_sold=, =main_crop=,
  crop-group shares are ALL =transformations= over that feature. Top gap —
  GAP_RANKING #1 area.

* 11. Ag valuations
- *Ours*: =food_prices= (runtime-derived from =food_acquired=) — *valuation
  logic only*, and only for *consumed food*, not plot production.
- *Theirs*: =harvest_value_{LCU,USD}=, =yield_value_{LCU,USD}=,
  =seed_value_{LCU,USD}=, =hired_labor_value_{LCU,USD}=,
  =inorganic_fertilizer_value_{LCU,USD}=, =harvest_sold_value=. All
  median-price imputed (price = sold_value/sold_kg, then median over
  EA/admin cells with ≥10 obs), then USD via CPI deflator + Atlas FX.
- *agg/item*: agg (median-price imputed valuation — the canonical aggregate).
- *.do source*: valuation programs =programs.do:4-108 (crops)=,
  =138-243 (seeds)=, =244-470 (wages)=, =471-561 (fert)=; deflator in each
  =Append_{CC}.do:~440-520=.
- *Recommendation*: BOTH^a in name only. *Never* store any =*_value_*= column.
  Once Areas 9 & 10 item features exist, add a single =transformations= fn
  =median_price_valuation()= that reproduces their EA/admin median-price
  ladder over the item rows + a =deflate_to_usd()= helper. This is the
  archetypal "aggregation lives in transformations" case. GAP_RANKING #7.
