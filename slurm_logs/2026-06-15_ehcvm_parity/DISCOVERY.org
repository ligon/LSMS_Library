* Per-country adaptation quirks (the build work; EHCVM template otherwise shared)
- Country code in filenames: ben/bfa/civ(UPPERCASE CIV)/gnb/sen/tgo, year 2018.
- Path layout differs: most are =2018-19/Data/=; CotedIvoire is =2018-19/Data/Menage/=;
  Togo is =2018/Data1/= (wave dir "2018", subdir "Data1").
- Benin has a DUPLICATE wave dir (=2018-19/= and =2018-2019/=) — data-hygiene flag; pick one.
- Burkina_Faso + Senegal also have a 2021-22 EHCVM round, but it LACKS s04 (use
  2018-19 for people_last7days, or treat 2021-22 separately).
- CotedIvoire lacks =plot_features= (predates the EHCVM-list per CLAUDE.md) — wire it from s16 too.
- Label foundation (=harmonize_food= + =u=) per country first (exists for several).

* Blocked / caveated
- *anthropometry*: BLOCKED.  EHCVM s05/s06 (Benin) carry NO body-measure
  columns (no poids/taille/weight/height); Mali/Niger had no anthropometry in
  the ISA pass either.  EHCVM does not field child anthropometry -> drop from
  the extension.
- *community_prices*: CAVEATED.  Niger _/community_prices.py notes the EHCVM
  community price data is "not at the grappe grain" and the ehcvm_nsu median is
  "NOT this" -> the EHCVM community-price grain is problematic.  Lower priority;
  confirm the usable _co_ price section before wiring.

* Work-list / build shape
- 5 features x 6 countries = 30 country-feature cells, ALL from the Niger
  template (clone + per-country path/code adaptation, data-driven).
- anthropometry dropped (no module); community_prices deferred (caveated).
- Approach: parity-loop style — label foundation per country (Unit#0), then one
  branch per feature, parallel per-country worktree agents (now enabled by #436),
  cold-build + is_this_feature_sane + adversary-verified + test-gated, merge-if-green.
- Suggested pilot: livestock (simplest grain (t,i,animal)) cloned across the 6
  to validate the EHCVM-clone flow, then fan out crop_production / plot_inputs /
  plot_labor / people_last7days.
