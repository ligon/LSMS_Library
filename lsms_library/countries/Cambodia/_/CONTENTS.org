#+title: Contents

Cambodia LSMS+ survey coverage and status.

Source: [[https://microdata.worldbank.org/index.php/catalog/4045][World Bank Microdata Library, Catalog #4045]]
(Cambodia Living Standards Measurement Study - Plus 2019-2020)

* Overview

Cambodia currently has a single wave (2019-20) from the LSMS-Plus
program.  The survey includes 33 data files (=hh_sec_1.dta= through
=hh_sec_20b.dta=, plus =individual_cover.dta=), covering household
demographics, food consumption, agriculture, health, education,
employment, assets, and more.

Three country-level features are implemented (=other_features=,
=household_characteristics=, =food_acquired=).  The build uses the
legacy Makefile/script path exclusively---there is no =data_scheme.yml=
and no =data_info.yml= at the country level, so Cambodia is not yet
accessible via the =Country()= API.

* Known data issues

** shocks — not available (2026-06-06)
Cambodia 2019-20 LSMS+ has **no shocks-and-coping roster**. A full scan of
every household section (5–20b) via variable labels found none. Note the
section list above is mislabeled: =hh_sec_13a/13b= are **migration** modules
(internal + international labor migration: destination, ISCO occupation,
earnings), NOT shocks; =hh_sec_11/12= are education/health, not assets/credit.
The only shock-adjacent content is per-member illness/injury in the health
module (not a household shock-type × coping roster). `shocks` is therefore not
implementable. Tracked in GitHub issue #352.
