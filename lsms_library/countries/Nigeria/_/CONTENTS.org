* Sampling Design

** Overview

The Nigeria General Household Survey--Panel (GHS-Panel) is an LSMS-ISA
panel survey with a two-stage stratified cluster design.  Six
geopolitical zones serve as strata; within each zone, enumeration areas
(EAs) are the primary sampling units (PSUs/clusters).  Households are
drawn within EAs.

Each wave collects data in two rounds: post-planting (PP, ~Q3) and
post-harvest (PH, ~Q1 of the following year).  The same households are
visited in both rounds.  The library uses the post-planting cover page
(=secta_plantingw{N}.dta=) as the sample frame because it comes first
and defines the household universe for the wave.

Starting with wave 4 (2018-19), a refreshment sample of new households
was drawn alongside the continuing panel.  Wave 5 (2023-24) continues
this rotating panel design.
